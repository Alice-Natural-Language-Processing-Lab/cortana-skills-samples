Microsoft Graph CSharp Example

Bot demonstrating calling Microsoft Graph after authenticating with Cortana's Connected Services. Note, as pre-work, you should have an Outlook account with several contacts created that have birthdays attached.

  1. Log into outlook
  2. Click the hamburger menu
  3. Select People
  4. Click +Add New Contact
  5. Fill in the form with contact details
  6. Click Add More -> Other -> Birthday
  7. Enter the birthday
  8. Save
After this, "Open Birthday Countdown" to get the top ten list of people with birthdays.
